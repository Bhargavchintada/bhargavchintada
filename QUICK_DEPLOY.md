## ⚡ FAST DEPLOYMENT (5 Minutes)

### Step 1: Deploy to Vercel (RECOMMENDED)
1. Go to https://vercel.com
2. Click "New Project"
3. Import GitHub: `Bhargavchintada/bhargavchintada`
4. Framework Preset: "Other"
5. Click "Deploy"

### Step 2: Add Custom Domain
1. In Vercel dashboard → "Domains"
2. Add: `bhargavchintada.me`
3. Vercel will show: **CNAME: cname.vercel-dns.com**

### Step 3: Update DNS at NS1
1. Go to https://my.nsone.net
2. Select domain: `bhargavchintada.me`
3. Click "Add Record"
4. Enter:
   - **Type**: CNAME
   - **Name**: @
   - **Value**: cname.vercel-dns.com
   - **TTL**: 300
5. Click "Save"

### Step 4: Wait & Test
- **5 minutes**: DNS propagation
- **Test**: https://bhargavchintada.me
